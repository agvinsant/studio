QR Codes

Have you ever seen those little black and white square codes that say scan with your phone for info?

Well, those are called QR codes. Like bar codes, they hold information.

QR codes, when scanned with an app from your mobile phone will send you to a website, call a number, send you to an email, or send you a text message.

Many people are using these to replace information on business cards, use as logos and put on shirts and car signs.

There are virtually endless uses for these little things. I can create and customize your QR codes to any look and feel you want. I can embed logos and images and use custom colors and backgrounds. I will then send you a jpeg file of your code for you to use anyway you would like.
